Our Story

Where Craft Meets Community

Based in Ocala, Florida, Precision Pour LLC was built on a simple belief — every event deserves professional, stress-free bar service. We're a fully insured, dry hire mobile bartending company bringing skill, precision, and personality to events of every size across Marion County and Central Florida.

Fully Insured
$1M per occurrence, $2M aggregate coverage through FLIP. Your event is protected.
Dry Hire Model
You supply the alcohol, we bring the expertise, the bar, and the experience.
Locally Rooted
Ocala born and community driven. We know the market and we love serving it.
Contract Provided
Every booking comes with a formal service agreement. Professional from day one.

The Process

Simple as One, Two, Three

01
Inquire & Consult
Fill out our contact form with your event details. We'll respond within 24 hours to discuss your vision, guest count, drink preferences, and answer any questions.
02
Review & Sign
We send your custom invoice and service contract. Review everything, sign the agreement, and pay your 30% deposit to officially secure your date.
03
Prepare Together
We work with you on your drink menu and signature cocktail selections. You handle the alcohol purchase — we give you a detailed shopping guide to make it easy.
04
We Show Up & Pour
On event day we arrive early, set up completely, and serve your guests professionally from start to finish. You focus on enjoying your event — we handle the bar.
